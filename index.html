About the Hackathon

Join us at Troy Hackathon 2024, A thrilling event tackling real-world challenges. Participants are encouraged to form teams and collaborate on developing solutions. Throughout the hackathon, industry experts and mentors will provide valuable support, guiding participants through workshops, and thought-provoking discussions. The event concludes with project presentations, where winners will be acknowledged and rewarded. Come be a part of this incredible journey and make a meaningful impact with your unique skills and creative ideas.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Troy Hack??
TroyHack is a 24-hour event where students gather to create awesome application.
What is the Topic?
The topic will be released in the opening ceremony on Oct 12th Saturday.Please don't start or work on your project.
How do I register for the Hackathon?
You can register for the Hackathon through our google forms like provided in the registration tab.
Who can participate in the Hackathon?
The Hackathon is open to all students, regardless of major or experience level. We encourage participants to form diverse teams and collaborate with students from different backgrounds.
Are there teams?
Working as a team is highly encouraged, but not required. We'll be facilitating team forming, so don't be discouraged if you don't have a team! We don't actually track team formations until project submission, where you'll list your crew and their contributions to code, design, and idea.
What if I don't have a team?
No problem! We will help you find a team on the day of the event, or you can participate as an individual.
How big can a team be?
Maximum of 4 people!
What should I bring to the event?
You should bring your own laptop, charger, and any other necessary equipment or software for your project. Food and drinks will be provided.
What are the prizes?
The prizes will be announced closer to the event date.
